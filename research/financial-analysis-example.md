# Financial Analysis Example: SaaS Companies

This example demonstrates how the competitive intelligence agent conducts comprehensive financial analysis, including both private and public company research.

## Example 1: Private Company Analysis - Stripe

### Company Overview
**Status**: Private
**Last Known Valuation**: $95 billion (March 2021)
**Industry**: Payment Processing

### Financial Research Process

#### Step 1: Funding History Research
**Search Queries Used**:
- `Stripe funding rounds valuation history`
- `Stripe Series A B C D investors`
- `Stripe $95 billion valuation 2021`

**Results Found**:
| Round | Date | Amount | Valuation | Lead Investor | Key Details |
|-------|------|--------|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Angel | 2011 | $2M | $20M | Y Combinator | Initial funding |
| Series A | 2012 | $18M | $100M | Sequoia Capital | Payment focus |
| Series B | 2014 | $70M | $1.75B | Khosla Ventures | International expansion |
| Series C | 2016 | $150M | $9.2B | CapitalG | Product expansion |
| Series D | 2018 | $245M | $20B | Tiger Global | Market leadership |
| Series G | 2019 | $250M | $35B | Andreessen Horowitz | Growth acceleration |
| Series H | 2021 | $600M | $95B | Fidelity | Record valuation |

**Total Funding**: ~$1.4 billion
**Key Investors**: Sequoia Capital, Andreessen Horowitz, Tiger Global, Fidelity

#### Step 2: Revenue & Business Metrics Research
**Search Queries Used**:
- `Stripe revenue 2024 annual recurring revenue`
- `Stripe payment volume TPV total payment volume`
- `Stripe profitability burn rate cash flow 2024`

**Estimated Financials**:
- **Estimated Revenue (2024)**: $18-20 billion
- **Revenue Growth**: 25-30% YoY
- **Total Payment Volume**: $1+ trillion annually
- **Take Rate**: ~2.9% + 30¢ per transaction
- **Profitability Status**: Approaching break-even
- **Estimated Burn Rate**: $200-300M/quarter
- **Cash Position**: Strong (~$2B+ raised)

#### Step 3: Business Health Assessment
**Strengths**:
- Market-leading position in online payments
- Strong revenue growth and improving unit economics
- Blue-chip investor backing
- Diversified product portfolio (Atlas, Climate, etc.)

**Risks**:
- High competition from PayPal, Square, Adyen
- Regulatory scrutiny in multiple markets
- Economic sensitivity (transaction volumes)
- High valuation relative to public comps

## Example 2: Public Company Analysis - Salesforce (CRM)

### Company Overview
**Status**: Public (NYSE: CRM)
**Market Cap**: ~$240 billion (as of 2024)
**Industry**: Cloud Software/CRM

### Financial Research Process

#### Step 1: SEC Filings Analysis
**Documents Reviewed**:
- 10-K Annual Report (2024, 2023, 2022)
- 10-Q Quarterly Reports (Q1-Q4 2024)
- 8-K Current Reports (major announcements)
- Earnings call transcripts (last 4 quarters)

#### Step 2: 3-Year Financial Analysis

##### Revenue Analysis
| Metric | FY2024 | FY2023 | FY2022 | 3-Yr CAGR |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| Total Revenue | $34.9B | $31.4B | $26.5B | 14.7% |
| Subscription Revenue | $32.5B | $29.2B | $24.7B | 14.6% |
| Revenue Growth | 11% | 18% | 25% | Decelerating |
| International Revenue | 30% | 29% | 28% | Stable |

##### Profitability & Margins
| Metric | FY2024 | FY2023 | FY2022 | Trend |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| Gross Margin | 76% | 75% | 74% | ↗️ Improving |
| Operating Margin | 2% | 3% | 2% | → Volatile |
| Net Margin | 3% | 1% | 1% | ↗️ Improving |
| Free Cash Flow | $6.2B | $6.0B | $4.8B | ↗️ Growing |

##### Key Business Metrics
| Metric | FY2024 | FY2023 | FY2022 | Trend |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| Customers | 150,000+ | 150,000+ | 150,000+ | → Stable |
| Average Contract Value | $233K | $210K | $195K | ↗️ Growing |
| Customer Acquisition Cost | ~$15K | ~$14K | ~$12K | ↗️ Rising |
| Net Revenue Retention | 105% | 108% | 114% | ↘️ Declining |

#### Step 3: Recent Earnings Analysis

**Q4 2024 Highlights**:
- Revenue: $9.29B (+11% YoY) vs. $9.35B guidance
- Key Product Updates: Einstein GPT, Slack AI integration
- Guidance: FY2025 revenue $37.7-$38.0B (+8-9% growth)
- Management Commentary: "AI transformation driving customer engagement"

**Q3 2024 Key Points**:
- Revenue: $8.72B (+11% YoY)
- Data Cloud revenue up 22% YoY
- 1,000+ Data Cloud deals closed
- Slack integration showing momentum

#### Step 4: Acquisition Activity
| Date | Target | Amount | Strategic Rationale | Integration Status |
|------|--------|---------|-------------------|-------------------|
| 2021 | Slack | $27.7B | Collaboration platform | Successful |
| 2019 | Tableau | $15.7B | Analytics/BI | Successful |
| 2018 | MuleSoft | $6.5B | Integration platform | Successful |
| 2016 | Demandware | $2.8B | E-commerce | Successful |

#### Step 5: Valuation Analysis
- **Current Market Cap**: $240B
- **Enterprise Value**: $235B
- **Price/Revenue Multiple**: 6.9x (TTM)
- **Price/Earnings Multiple**: 45x (TTM)
- **Peer Comparison**: Premium to Microsoft (5.2x), ServiceNow (12x)

#### Step 6: Financial Health Assessment
**Balance Sheet Strength**:
- **Cash & Equivalents**: $13.2B
- **Total Debt**: $11.1B
- **Debt-to-Equity Ratio**: 0.4 (healthy)
- **Current Ratio**: 1.1 (adequate)
- **Financial Risk Level**: Low

**Key Financial Risks**:
1. **Growth Deceleration**: Revenue growth slowing from 25% to 11%
2. **Margin Pressure**: High competition impacting pricing power
3. **Integration Challenges**: Large acquisitions (Slack, Tableau) still integrating
4. **Economic Sensitivity**: Enterprise spending vulnerable to downturn

### Investment Thesis
**Bull Case**:
- Market leader with strong competitive moats
- AI integration creating new growth opportunities
- Strong free cash flow generation
- Successful track record of acquisitions

**Bear Case**:
- Slowing growth and high valuation multiples
- Intense competition from Microsoft, Google, others
- Complex product portfolio may confuse customers
- Macro headwinds affecting enterprise spending

**Analyst Consensus**: Hold with $285 average price target

## Financial Analysis Search Strategies

### For Private Companies:
```
"[Company]" funding round Series A B C valuation
"[Company]" revenue 2024 ARR annual recurring revenue
"[Company]" investors Sequoia Andreessen Horowitz Tiger Global
"[Company]" burn rate profitability cash flow
"[Company]" valuation $XX billion unicorn
site:crunchbase.com "[Company]" funding
site:pitchbook.com "[Company]" private markets
```

### For Public Companies:
```
"[Company]" 10-K annual report 2024 filetype:pdf
"[Company]" earnings call transcript Q4 2024
"[Company]" investor presentation deck 2024
"[Company]" revenue growth margin profitability
site:sec.gov "[Company]" 10-Q quarterly report
site:investor.company.com earnings financial results
```

### Key Financial Metrics to Track:

#### Growth Metrics:
- Revenue growth (YoY, 3-year CAGR)
- Customer growth and retention
- Average contract value trends
- Market share expansion

#### Profitability Metrics:
- Gross margin trends
- Operating leverage
- Free cash flow generation
- Path to profitability (for unprofitable companies)

#### Financial Health:
- Cash position and burn rate
- Debt levels and leverage ratios
- Working capital management
- Capital allocation strategy

#### Valuation Metrics:
- Revenue multiples vs peers
- Growth-adjusted valuation (PEG)
- Enterprise value considerations
- Market cap vs addressable market

This comprehensive financial analysis enables strategic decision-making around competitive positioning, market entry, partnership opportunities, and investment considerations.
